Developed as a tool for Primary School Teachers. Up to ten individual colours can be chosen. Any probability out of ten can be chosen by entering colours proportionally. E.g. Red, Red, Green will draw a chart with two thirds Red and one third Green. Once chosen, a colour's "pie piece" is changed to white to indicate choice. It is assumed that users will type mainstream colours that are more likely to be supported e.g. magenta instead of rose. All blues are displayed as one hues/shade intentionally for better visibility.

N.b. Currently only maximised views of this page are supported.
